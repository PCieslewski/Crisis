What can Crisis do?




View Your Schedule


Go to the "My Schedule" tab to view your schedule. By using your GatorLink credentials, the developers of Crisis get your class schedule right off ISIS.



Add/Remove Events


On the "My Schedule" tab simply click any box on your calendar. If an event is present a pop up will be displayed to allow you to remove that event. If the time slot is empty a pop up will be displayed allowing you to enter the title, building, and room of an event.
Plan Meetings


To plan a meeting go to the "Friends" tab. Check the friends you want to be apart of the meeting. Then check the box to view your own schedule. Now just click on any available space on the table and fill in the information to send those friends a meeting request.
